Calculate Your BMI

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using height and weight to estimate whether a person is underweight, normal, overweight, or obese. It’s widely used as a quick screening tool to understand general weight categories.

Precaution: BMI does not measure body fat or overall health accurately. Do not rely on it alone—always consider other health factors and consult a medical professional for proper assessment.
